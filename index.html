About Zachary

Raised in Fairfeild County, Zachary grew up as an active member of Cedar Point Yacht Club. Competitively racing Lightnings, Lasers and V15's on the Long Island Sound. Also racing on the Salve Regina University Sailing Team, he was active on the College circuit. In his past time, he enjoys going up to Okemo Mountain to ski with his family and also enjoys playing Rock and classical music on the piano.
